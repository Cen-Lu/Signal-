Signal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

Overview

Signal is built on the principle that your productivity data should remain private and under your complete control. This privacy policy explains exactly what data we collect, how we handle it, and why we made these privacy-first choices.

Our Privacy Philosophy

Privacy by Design: We can't misuse data we don't have. Signal is architecturally designed to collect minimal data and store everything locally on your device.

Transparency: This policy is written in plain English. No legal jargon designed to hide our practices.

User Control: You own your data completely. You can export it, delete it, or take it with you at any time.

What Data We Collect

Goals and Notes (Local Only)

  • What: Your signals (goals), descriptions, priority levels, completion status, and notes
  • Where Stored: Exclusively on your device using iOS Core Data
  • Our Access: Zero. We cannot read, access, or retrieve your goal content
  • Encryption: Protected by iOS device encryption and your device passcode
  • Backup: Not backed up to our servers (intentionally)

No Usage Analytics

Signal does not collect usage analytics or statistics.

What We Don't Track:

  • ❌ Your specific goals or notes content
  • ❌ Number of signals created or feature usage
  • ❌ App crashes or performance data
  • ❌ Personal identifiers or device IDs
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ User behavior or session recordings
  • ❌ Contact information or device contacts

Zero Data Collection: We have chosen not to implement analytics to maintain complete privacy

Purchase Information

  • What: Confirmation that you've purchased Signal Pro
  • Source: Apple App Store provides purchase verification only
  • Our Access: We only know "this device has a valid pro purchase" - no payment details, names, or financial information
  • Apple's Role: All payment processing handled by Apple under their privacy policy

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Device Only: All your personal data stays on your iPhone
  • iOS Protection: Data encrypted using iOS standard encryption
  • No Cloud Sync: Intentionally no iCloud or remote backup of goal content
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all your data

No Server Storage

  • No Analytics: We don't collect or store any usage analytics
  • No User Data: We have no servers storing your personal information
  • Purchase Verification Only: Only Apple's purchase confirmation is processed

Your Privacy Rights

Access

  • Local Data: All your data is visible within the app - no hidden information
  • No Analytics: We don't collect any analytics data, so there's nothing to access

Control

  • Edit: Modify or delete any signal or note anytime
  • Export: Use iOS share sheet to export your goals as text
  • No Tracking: No analytics to opt out of - we don't track anything

Deletion

  • Individual Goals: Delete specific signals within the app
  • All Local Data: Uninstall the app to permanently remove all your personal data
  • Purchase Records: Apple retains purchase information for your Apple ID (outside our control)
  • Support Communications: Email us to request deletion of any support correspondence
  • Response Time: 72 hours maximum for deletion requests

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Signal is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We don't collect personal information from users of any age, including children under 13
  • Local Storage Only: All user data stays on the device, never transmitted to our servers
  • No Registration Required: No accounts, emails, or personal information needed
  • Parent-Friendly: Parents can review the app's simple functionality and complete privacy approach

Summary

What makes Signal different:

  • Your goals never leave your device
  • We can't read your personal content
  • No analytics tracking
  • One-time purchase, no ongoing data relationship
  • Built for privacy from the ground up

Bottom line: We've architected Signal so that protecting your privacy isn't just a policy promise—it's a technical impossibility for us to violate it.
